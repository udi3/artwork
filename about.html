UDAYA LAKSHMI

I like looking at things. It takes me a while to stop looking, so I started sketching as a cover for when I'd step too close to a flower or stare too long at a building. I don't do this with people. I'd rather talk to them :)

Ink is my favorite plaything. I've played with charcoal and pencils but I keep going back to ink. It lets me bend the way things can be seen. I also love watercolors for their graceful simplicity.

While I post most of the bite-size art I make nowadays, this remains my diary of seeing.
